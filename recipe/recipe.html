The Greatest Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe That's Ever Lived

Cookies

Ingredients

  • 8 tablespoons of salted butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup of chocolate chips

Directions

  • 1. Preheat oven to 350 Degrees
  • 2. Cream together the butter, white sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips and nuts (optional). Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  • 3. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned
